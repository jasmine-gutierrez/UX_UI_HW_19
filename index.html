Welcome

I'm Jasmine Gutierrez

I'm a passionate UX/UI designer dedicated to promoting diversity and inclusion in tech. With experience mentoring Latina high school students and leading research in STEM support, I've seen the impact of user-centric design firsthand. My mission is to create inclusive digital experiences that reflect diverse perspectives. I'm committed to accessible, user-friendly, and aesthetically pleasing interfaces. Let's collaborate to make positive changes through design.

My UX Skills

User Research

I rely on User Research methods like interviews, surveys, and usability testing to gather insights directly from users. These invaluable insights guide my design choices, ensuring every element of a product caters to user needs and preferences. User Research helps me bridge the gap between user expectations and product design, resulting in user-centric and delightful experiences.

User Interface Design

User interface design is my creative tool to make technology user-friendly. I transform complex ideas into visually appealing and intuitive digital experiences, prioritizing user needs and behavior. Whether it's a website or an app, I aim to create designs that are both functional and visually engaging.

Web Development

I love using web development to bring creative ideas to life on the internet. By combining my design and coding skills, I create interactive and visually appealing websites that engage users. Whether it's crafting user-friendly interfaces or optimizing site performance, I enjoy every step of the process in making the web more vibrant and accessible.

My Work

North American Butterfly Association Redesign

The NABA redesign project aims to transform the online presence of the North American Butterfly Association into a captivating and user-centric experience. Through user research, we will gather insights on pain points and challenges faced by stakeholders of NABA, and aim to solve these through a website redesign.

Skippr.

The Skippr. App project aims to understand our users experience navigating the grocery store with the intention of identifying the pain points and areas of improvement to enhance their overall grocery shopping experience.

Empowering Underrepresented People in UX/UI

Empowering Underrepresented Individuals in UX/UI Project aims to find the challenges that hinder their full participation and success when breaking into the field. With a limited number of organizations devoted to all/any underrepresented communities in UX specifically, we aim to understand our market and establish a pathway for our project through research.
